Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

HeartLab ("we," "our," "us," or "Company") operates the HeartLab website and application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our Platform.

IMPORTANT: HeartLab is built on principles of ANONYMITY and TRANSPARENCY. We explain in detail what data we collect and how we use it. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, full name
  • Address Information: Street address, city, state/province, ZIP/postal code, country
  • Social Media Profiles: Facebook, Twitter, or other social media handles (if you connect them)
  • Payment Information: Billing details (processed securely through Razorpay)
  • Session Data: Content shared during voice calls with AI companions
  • Feedback: Surveys, reviews, and user feedback

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used, session duration
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies
  • Audio Data: Voice recordings during sessions (processed through Vapi)

2. Analytics & Tracking Tools

2.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our Platform. Data includes pages visited, time spent, device information, and geographic location.

  • Google Analytics Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Opt-Out: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

2.2 Other Analytics Tools

We may use: Hotjar, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Facebook Pixel, and other analytics services to improve our Platform.

2.3 Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential cookies (required for functionality)
  • Analytics cookies (usage tracking)
  • Marketing cookies (personalized ads)
  • Performance cookies (technical optimization)

You can manage cookies through our cookie consent panel or your browser settings.

3. Email Communications

3.1 Types of Emails

Transactional Emails (Required):

  • Session confirmations and reminders
  • Payment receipts
  • Account updates and security alerts
  • Important Platform announcements

Marketing Emails (Opt-In):

  • Promotions and special offers
  • New features and announcements
  • Blog posts and wellness tips
  • Event invitations

3.2 Unsubscribe

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any email. You cannot unsubscribe from transactional emails (receipts, confirmations).

4. Advertising & Ads

4.1 How We Use Ads

We display personalized advertisements to monetize the Platform and support our services. Ads appear through:

  • Google Ads (Google AdSense, Display Network)
  • Facebook Ads
  • Other ad networks and partners

4.2 Personalized Advertising

We use your information for personalized ads based on:

  • Your interests and behavior
  • Your demographics (age, location, gender)
  • Your browsing history (through cookies)

4.3 Opting Out

You can opt-out of personalized ads:

  • Google Ad Settings: https://myaccount.google.com/data-and-privacy
  • Facebook Ads: https://www.facebook.com/ads/preferences/
  • Use ad-blocking extensions (uBlock Origin, AdBlock)

5. Payment Processing

5.1 Razorpay

We use Razorpay for secure payment processing. Your payment information is handled by Razorpay, not stored on our servers.

  • Razorpay Privacy: https://razorpay.com/privacy/
  • Payment Data Retention: 7 years (tax compliance)
  • Security: Bank-level encryption, PCI-DSS compliance

6. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Providing HeartLab access, processing payments, sending confirmations
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns, fixing bugs, optimizing performance
  • Marketing: Sending promotions, displaying personalized ads, newsletters
  • Analytics: Understanding user demographics and behavior, measuring effectiveness
  • Legal: Complying with laws, responding to legal requests, fraud prevention
  • Crisis Intervention: In urgent situations, we may contact emergency services for your safety

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive data in standard format
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of marketing or processing
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File complaint with your Data Protection Authority

9.2 DPDP Act Rights (India Users)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct or update your information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent
  • File grievance complaints

9.3 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you're in California, you have rights under CCPA/CPRA:

  • Right to know what data we collect
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to opt-out of data sales
  • Right to non-discrimination

16. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does HeartLab store my conversations?
A: Your voice sessions are processed in real-time and deleted after each session. We do NOT keep permanent records of your conversations.
Q: Will you sell my data?
A: No. We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share aggregate, anonymized data for research and analytics.
Q: Can I delete my account and data?
A: Yes. You can request account deletion by emailing privacy@openmindsnovatech.com. Most data will be deleted within 30 days. Tax records are retained for 7 years.
Q: Will I receive marketing emails?
A: You may receive marketing emails if opted in. Unsubscribe anytime by clicking the link in any marketing email.
Q: How do I opt-out of personalized ads?
A: Visit Google Ad Settings (https://myaccount.google.com/ads-preferences) or disable cookies in your browser settings.
Q: Is my payment information safe?
A: Yes. Razorpay handles payment processing with bank-level encryption. We do NOT store your complete card details.
Q: What are my rights under GDPR?
A: GDPR users have rights to access, rectification, erasure, data portability, and objection. Exercise rights by emailing privacy@openmindsnovatech.com.
Q: What happens in a crisis?
A: HeartLab is not a crisis service. If you express immediate danger, we may contact emergency services. Please reach out to crisis helplines immediately.
